## ANTI-RAGGING POLICY

### Definition
"Ragging" means any act which causes, or is likely to cause, any physical, psychological or physiological harm, apprehension, shame, or embarrassment to a student.

### Types of Ragging (Strictly Prohibited)
- **Physical Abuse:** Forcing to eat, drink, smoke, or forcing to dress/undress
- **Verbal Abuse:** Swear words, derogatory references to appearance, attire, religion, caste, family, or field of study
- **Forced Activities:** Chores for seniors (copying notes, cleaning rooms, etc.)
- **Restriction:** Not being allowed to attend classes
- **Sleep Deprivation:** Staying awake late or getting up at unreasonable times
- **Forced Performance:** Singing, dancing, or performing in any other way
- **Behavioral Misconduct:** Using foul language, shouting, cheering loudly, or misbehaving with strangers, particularly women
