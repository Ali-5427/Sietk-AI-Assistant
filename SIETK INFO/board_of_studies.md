# BOARD OF STUDIES

The Board of Studies (BoS) is the basic constituent of academic system of the institution. Its
functions include framing the syllabi for various courses, reviewing and updating syllabi from
time to time, introducing new courses of study, determining details of continuous assessment,
recommending panels of examiners under the semester system and all other matters related to
good governance of academics.


# Composition of BoS

Board of studies of every department shall be constituted as per the UGC guidelines


| CATEGORY | STATUS |
| --- | --- |
| Head of the department concerned. | Chairman |
| The entire faculty of each specialization. | Member |
| Two experts in the subject from outside the institution to be nominated by the Academic Council. | Member |
| One expert to be nominated by the vice-chancellor from a panel of six recommended by the institution Principal. | Member |
| One representative from industry/corporate sector/allied area relating to placement. | Member |
| One postgraduate meritorious alumnus to be nominated by the Principal. | Member |
| The chairman, Board of Studies, may with the approval of the principal of the institution, co-opt <br> (a) Experts from outside the institution whenever special courses of studies are to be formulated. <br> (b) Other members of staff of the same faculty. | Member |
